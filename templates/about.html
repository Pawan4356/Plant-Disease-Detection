About Plant Disease Detection System

Overview

Our Plant Disease Detection System is an AI-powered tool designed to help farmers and agricultural professionals detect plant diseases quickly and accurately. By uploading an image of a plant leaf, users can identify diseases affecting their crops and receive actionable solutions to manage them.

Supported Crops

The model is trained to detect diseases in the following plants: Tomato, Grape, Orange, Soybean, Squash, Potato, Corn (Maize), Strawberry, Peach, Apple, Blueberry, Cherry (including Sour), Bell Pepper, Raspberry.

Detected Diseases

The system can identify the following diseases across different plant species:

  • Tomato: Late Blight, Early Blight, Septoria Leaf Spot, Yellow Leaf Curl Virus, Bacterial Spot, Target Spot, Leaf Mold, Spider Mites (Two-Spotted), Tomato Mosaic Virus
  • Grape: Black Rot, Leaf Blight (Isariopsis Leaf Spot), Esca (Black Measles), Healthy
  • Apple: Apple Scab, Black Rot, Cedar Apple Rust, Healthy
  • Potato: Late Blight, Early Blight, Healthy
  • Corn (Maize): Northern Leaf Blight, Cercospora Leaf Spot (Gray Leaf Spot), Common Rust, Healthy
  • Strawberry: Leaf Scorch, Healthy
  • Peach: Bacterial Spot, Healthy
  • Orange: Haunglongbing (Citrus Greening)
  • Squash: Powdery Mildew
  • Cherry (including Sour): Powdery Mildew, Healthy
  • Bell Pepper: Bacterial Spot, Healthy
  • Soybean: Healthy
  • Blueberry: Healthy
  • Raspberry: Healthy
